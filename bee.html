Project Overview

This is my personal logo design. My Chinese name is Dimi（滴蜜）, which means a drop of honey. It is taken from the Bible. Honey is often praised as a beautiful thing in the Bible. The industrious bee is also a symbol of industriousness and competence. In the animation, a little bee is carrying honey, working hard, and fulfilling his own value. This has become my belief.

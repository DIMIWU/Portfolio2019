Project Overview

Linker is a communication platform for designers and connect designers. As designers, we need a platform to connect with each other and communicate with more people, which will help to improve design skill and thought. This App, which is based on career orientation, builds Digital Design groups or organizations. Whether you are a student or a senior designer, you can make connections on this platform. Get advice for work efficiency or career direction and so on.
